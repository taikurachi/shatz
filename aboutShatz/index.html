ABOUT US

Matthew Shatz is the Managing Principal & Founder of Shatz Capital.

Founded in Q3 2023, Shatz Capital is a Miami Based Boutique Private Equity & Debt Advisory Firm specializing in commercial real estate investment and capital market transactions.

Prior to founding Shatz Capital, Matthew was Co-Head of the Debt & Structured Finance division at RIPCO Real Estate, where he launched the vertical nationally for the NY-based brokerage. Before joining RIPCO, Matthew was a Managing Director at B6 Real Estate Advisors’ NY-based Capital Advisory practice which he joined in 2019. In 2020 Matthew was recognized by B6 with their Top Producer award, leading all Capital Advisory agents in origination volume. He was additionally recognized by Commercial Observer as one of the “Top 25 Under 35 Professionals in Commercial Real Estate Finance”. Leading up to B6, Matthew spent several years at a NY-based boutique private equity and advisory shop where he executed several billion dollars in successful commercial real estate financings. Prior to his move into the advisory space, Shatz was on the principal investment side of the business as an Associate at Related Companies, after spending half a decade in Investment Banking at JP Morgan where he began his career.
