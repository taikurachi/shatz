WHY SHATZ CAPITAL

OUR DISTINCTIVE EGDE.

Proven Capital Market Experience

With over 15 years of experience, more than 100 transactions, and in excess of $7 billion in successful advisory and buy-side transactions, Shatz Capital boasts a track record that spans all commercial real estate (CRE) asset and transaction types. Our breadth of experience ensures sophisticated and knowledgeable handling of each deal, aligning with our clients' diverse needs.

Certainty of Execution

Shatz Capital partners exclusively with institutional-caliber capital partners, each with a proven closing track record. Our vast network includes national, regional, and local commercial and investment banks, private equity and debt funds, and government-sponsored entities. This expansive network is a testament to our ability to close with certainty.

Speed of Execution & Deep Relationships

Direct access to decision-makers is critical. Our enduring relationships with key personnel across our capital providers allow us to connect directly with principals and C-level executives, ensuring that every transaction is a priority from inception to close.

We Are Advisors, Not Brokers

Consider the Shatz Capital team as your comprehensive capital advisory firm. We're not just brokers; we're with you at every step, from initial underwriting to post-closing. Our involvement includes term sheet and loan document negotiation, coordination of 3rd party reporting, and regular updates to ensure a seamless closing process.

Targeted & Tailored Approach

Your deal is shown only to the most suitable set of capital partners, whose lending mandates align with your financing needs. Lenders know that a deal from Shatz Capital means a perfect fit for their platform, ensuring that your transaction receives focused attention and resources. This targeted method fosters a competitive market among the ideal lenders and ensures rapid, dedicated feedback without overexposing your deal.
